Welcome to Pay Roulette!

Why play?

Want to make your night out with friends more exciting? Well... you’ve come to the right spot!

Decide which friend should pay for the night out with a game of credit-card roulette.

How to play?

  • each players take turn in selecting a card of their choice
  • if a card is safe, you will get a green check
  • if it’s not, then you will get a red cross! This player must pay for everyone’s meal (or whatever forfeit)
